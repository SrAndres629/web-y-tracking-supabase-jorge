## 2025-03-19 - Accessibility for Icon-Only Elements
**Learning:** Icon-only elements, such as social links and mobile menu close buttons, must include an `aria-label` attribute on the parent tag and `aria-hidden="true"` on the inner icon element to ensure accessibility for screen readers.
**Action:** Always verify icon-only buttons and links have appropriate `aria-label` and `aria-hidden="true"` attributes.
